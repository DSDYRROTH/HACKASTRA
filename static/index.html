About Sign Language Detection

This application allows you to practice and detect sign language gestures using your webcam. It uses computer vision to recognize hand gestures representing the American Sign Language (ASL) alphabet.

Hover over any letter below to see the corresponding gesture. Click on letters to build a message in the practice area.

How It Works

  1. Click "Start Detection" to begin.
  2. Position your hand in view of your webcam.
  3. Make sign language gestures for alphabets A-Z.
  4. The application will detect and display the corresponding letter with its sign.
  5. Use the practice area above to try building words by clicking on letters.
  6. Hover over any letter to see how to sign it.
